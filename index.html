FAQs

常见问题

How long do alterations take? 修改一般需要多久？

Most basic alterations take 1–3 days. Complex work may take longer.

基础修改一般 1–3 天，复杂项目可能更久。

Do you offer rush service? 可以加急吗？

Yes, depending on workload and complexity. Please contact us for availability.

可以，需视排期与复杂度而定。欢迎联系我们确认。

Can you help source fabric? 可以帮忙找面料吗？

We can recommend fabric options and suggest where to purchase based on your design.

我们可按设计建议面料，并推荐购买渠道。

Do you require a deposit? 需要付订金吗？

For custom orders, a deposit is typically required to confirm your booking.

定制订单通常需要订金以确认预约与排期。

Are there limitations to alterations? 修改会有限制吗？

Some garments have limited seam allowance or structure that affects what’s possible. We’ll advise after inspection.

部分服装因缝份或结构限制可修改范围，我们会检查后给出建议。
